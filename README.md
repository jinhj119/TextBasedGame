A text-based adventure game written in Python.  
Collect items across the Star Kingdom and defeat the evil ruler Balthasar before it's too late!

Game Overview

In this adventure game, the player travels through different rooms in the Star Kingdom to collect six essential items needed to defeat Balthasar, an evil snake-like ruler who has taken control of the kingdom.

The player can:

- Move between rooms using:
  - `go North`
  - `go South`
  - `go East`
  - `go West`
- Collect items using:
  - `get <item name>`

The player wins by collecting all six items before entering the villain’s room.  
If the player enters the villain’s room without all required items, the game ends in defeat.

Rooms

- Starlight Dining Room (Start)
- Starbright Garden
- Stoney Cellar
- Crystal Bridge
- Brush Peak
- Elderwood Library
- Rainbow Den
- Sky Summit (Villain Room)

Items to Collect

- Shield of Dawn
- Guardian's Blade
- Challenger's Armor
- King's Helmet
- Guardian's Hammer
- Hero's Potion
